Starbright: Extended Edition Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy: August 1, 2016

Welcome to the privacy policy for Starbright: Extended Edition, created by Lunar Rabbit Games. This policy describes the type of data collected from your phone and how it is used within the application.

All information collected by Starbright: Extended Edition is controlled by Lunar Rabbit Games. By playing Starbright: Extended Edition, you are agreeing to allow the information gathered to be used for purposes as stated below.

The only data that Starbright: Extended Edition gathers is your Google Play user name and your scores. This name and the associated scores are used to display your name on the global high scores board. No other information about your Google Play Account or device will be collected or shown in any way.
