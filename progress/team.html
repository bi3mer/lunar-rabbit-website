Website

Laura Mo is a Computer Science student at Drexel University and one of the developers and designers at Lunar Rabbit Games. She decided that she would make video games after she played Pokemon Sapphire for 15 minutes in fourth grade and someone hasn't changed her mind since. She is fascinated by procedural generation and computer simulations for water. In her spare time she enjoys writing, drawing, sleeping, and eating.
